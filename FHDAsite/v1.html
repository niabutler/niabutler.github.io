About

A local dance studio specialising in RAD Ballet and NZAMD Jazz. We also offer contemporary and hip hop classes and private tuition. Classes taught by Fiona Haines, Chantelle Scoon, Emma Maddock and Stephanie Lee. Classes in Karori. Ballet, jazz, contemporary and hip-hop classes ages 3-18, 100% exam pass rate, experienced and motivating teachers, fantastic shows and fun dance classes in Karori and Brooklyn

Teachers

Fiona Haines - Ballet, Chantelle Scoon - Jazz, Emma Maddock - Junior Jazz and contemporary, Kowhai Jones - Hip Hop
