About

We are a local dance studio specialising in RAD Ballet and NZAMD Jazz. We also offer contemporary and hip hop classes and private tuition. The classes are taught by Fiona Haines, Chantelle Scoon and Emma Maddock. Classes in Karori. Ballet, jazz, contemporary and hip-hop classes ages 3-18, 100% exam pass rate, experienced and motivating teachers, fantastic shows and fun dance classes in Karori.
